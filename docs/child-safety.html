ZumiTok Child Safety Standards

Last updated: April 2026

Our Commitment

ZumiTok is committed to providing a safe environment for all users and has a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content that exploits or endangers minors.

Age Requirement

ZumiTok requires all users to be at least 13 years of age. Users under 18 must have parental consent. We do not knowingly allow children under 13 to create accounts.

Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited on ZumiTok:

  • Any content that sexually exploits or endangers minors
  • Content that depicts, promotes, or facilitates child abuse
  • Any attempt to solicit minors for inappropriate purposes

Reporting

Users can report any content or behavior that endangers children directly within the app using the Report feature, or by emailing il_shapiro@hotmail.com with the subject line "Child Safety Report".

We review all reports promptly and report confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant authorities.

Enforcement

Accounts found to violate our child safety policy will be immediately suspended and reported to the appropriate authorities.

Contact

For child safety concerns, contact us at il_shapiro@hotmail.com.
