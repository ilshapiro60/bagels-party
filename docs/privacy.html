ZumiTok privacy policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

This policy describes how ZumiTok (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application and related services. By using ZumiTok, you agree to this policy.

Who we are

ZumiTok is operated by the developer/entity responsible for the app in App Store Connect and Google Play. For questions: support@zumitok.app

What we collect

  • Account data: authentication identifiers and profile information you provide (such as display name and neighborhood area).
  • Pet & social content: pet profiles, photos, messages, posts, comments, and other content you choose to upload or send.
  • Location-related information: general area or neighborhood features you enable to show nearby content; we do not sell your precise real-time location.
  • Technical data: device type, app version, and diagnostic data needed to run and secure the service.

How we use information

  • Provide accounts, profiles, messaging, neighborhood features, and meetups.
  • Keep the community safer (for example moderation, abuse reports, and enforcing rules).
  • Improve reliability, fix bugs, and analyze aggregate usage.
  • Comply with law and respond to lawful requests.

Service providers

We use trusted infrastructure providers, which may process data on our behalf, including for example:

  • Google Firebase / Google Cloud (authentication, database, storage, notifications, and related backend services).
  • Apple & Google (app distribution, sign-in where offered, and platform services).
  • Maps and location providers where map features are used.
  • Payments or ads partners only if those features are enabled in your build (for example Stripe or ad networks), subject to their policies.

Update this list to match the SDKs and vendors you actually ship in production.

Retention & deletion

We keep information as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations. You may be able to delete your account or certain content in the app or by contacting support, subject to technical and legal constraints.

Children

ZumiTok is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from young children.

International users

Your information may be processed in the United States and other countries where providers operate.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new date at the top when we do.
